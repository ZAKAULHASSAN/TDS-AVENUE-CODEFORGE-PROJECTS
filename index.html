Tribute to Imran Khan

Why I Admire Imran Khan

Imran Khan, born in 1952, is a former international cricketer who led Pakistan to its first Cricket World Cup victory in 1992. Transitioning to politics, he founded the Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) in 1996, eventually becoming the country's Prime Minister from 2018 to 2022. Known for his philanthropy, particularly in healthcare and education, Khan has been a vocal advocate for anti-corruption measures and social reform throughout his career.
